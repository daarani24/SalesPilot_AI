PRICING & PAYMENT POLICIES
=====================================================

EMI Options: Available on all products above ₹15,000. No-cost EMI for
3, 6, and 9 month tenures on select bank cards.

Discounts:
- Students get 5% discount with valid ID on laptops.
- Bulk orders (5+ units) for businesses get 10% discount, contact sales team.
- Festival season offers up to 12% off on select premium models (seasonal, ask sales team for current status).

Warranty: All laptops come with 1-year manufacturer warranty.
All mobile phones come with 1-year manufacturer warranty + 6 months
TechHub extended screen protection.

Replacement Policy: 7-day no-questions-asked replacement if the device
has a manufacturing defect.

Delivery: Free delivery within city limits. 2-4 business days for
other locations within India.

=====================================================
COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS
=====================================================

Q: Can I upgrade RAM or storage later?
A: Most laptops in our Mid and Premium tier support RAM/storage upgrades
except MacBook Air (Apple devices have soldered/fixed memory, not
upgradeable after purchase).

Q: Do you buy back old devices?
A: Yes, TechHub offers an exchange program. Exchange value depends on
device age and condition, evaluated in-store.

Q: Is financing available for students without credit history?
A: Yes, TechHub partners with select NBFCs for student financing plans
requiring only ID proof and college enrollment proof.
